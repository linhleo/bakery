Our Story

Linh Bakery was founded in 2020 by Linh Nguyen. She has nurtured her passion for baking since she was far away from her home country and had to learn how to bake some Asian special cakes for herself. After being awarded a Marketing Bachelor degree from The University of Wollongong in 2019, she decided to leave her academic career behind and started doing something that she was really into. After hundreds of failures in her home kitchen, Linh has eventually come up with many creative delicious baking recipes which satisfy people from different cultures: Aussies, Asian, European,...and is proud to open her very first bakery in Australia. Besides Western cakes, she also introduces many special Asian cakes to bring Asian cuisine beyond just savoury meals. Whether you are a foreigner wanting to try Asian baking style, or an Asian missing your Mom's cakes, you are more than welcome here. Our cakes are baked with lots of love, pride and caring.
