Our Story

How we started

Linh's Bakery was founded in 2020 by Linh Nguyen. She has nurtured her passion for baking since she was far away from her home country and had to learn how to bake some Asian cakes for herself. After being awarded a Marketing Bachelor degree from The University of Wollongong in 2019, she decided to leave her academic career behind and started doing something that she was really into. After hundreds of failures in her home kitchen, Linh has eventually come up with many creative delicious baking recipes which satisfy people from different cultures: Aussies, Asian, European,...and is proud to open her very first bakery in Australia. Besides Western cakes, she also introduces many special Asian cakes to bring Asian cuisine beyond just savoury meals. Whether you are a foreigner wanting to try Asian baking style, or an Asian missing your Mom's cakes, you are more than welcome here. Our cakes are baked with lots of love, pride and caring.

What we make

We offer a wide range of cakes, from very wide-known ones such as Cheesecakes, Cupcakes, Egg Tarts,...to special Asian style cakes such as Castella, Sponge Cake with Salted Egg sauce, Taiwanese Pineapple Cake,... We also have special deep fried glutinous rice ball cakes (with red been or coconut mung bean filling) on every Thursday, Saturday and Sunday. Please have a look at our menu to see what we are offering or contact us if you have any enquiries on product availabilty. We also offer to make special cakes for special occasions such as wedding, birthday,...which are tailored to your taste and preference.

Where our ingredients are from

At Linh's Bakery, our number 1 priority is food safety. Our ingredients are fresh and chosen very carefully by Linh herself to guarantee the high quality of our cakes.
